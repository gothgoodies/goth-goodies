The Man Behind The Mask

I'm Hannah, the person behind the shadows. Jack of all trades, everything can be made to order. I just love designing and crafting. I've been doing everything under the 'art' moon since I was just a little girl. It all started with loom bands. Crafting was my middle name. Always. It was always me and my best friend Lily Zimdars, who very sadly passed on in April of 2020. We made so many loom band bracelets and charms and just everything. We took sewing classes together in summer school. She helped me find my soul purpose — CREATE🤍. Everything I do is with love and intention. I do not ask for much money because it just means so much to me and that's not what I'm asking for. I've been busting my ass to make a career for myself. A better life for me and my fiancé, Ethan. I strive to make him and myself proud of, well, myself! And it's all about vibes! Good energy! I found my love for horror and gothy stuff in middle school when I became depressed. I've worked my way out of the deep pit, into the forest of the unknown. Today, finding everything in my path, I use it to create something beautiful.
