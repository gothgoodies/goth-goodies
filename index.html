The Man Behind The Mask

   

I’ve loved art since I was just a girl (not a vamp yet). One of my earliest pieces was inspired by Andy Warhol, featuring a viola I played in elementary school. Each square had color opposites — like I knew balance in chaos even back then. This piece was created for the Mary Hoard Art Museum.

       

I also used to work at Skully’s Terror Haunted House (Jefferson County Fairgrounds) every October in 2022 & 2023 — first as a clown in the blacklight room, then as a hidden skeleton behind cages. Haunted history runs deep in my bones.
